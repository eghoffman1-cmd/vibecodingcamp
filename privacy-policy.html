Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Be Anything ("the Game") is a browser-based city simulation game. This privacy policy explains how we handle information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The Game runs entirely on your device. No accounts, no sign-ups, no data sent to any server.

Data Storage

The Game does not use cookies, local storage, or any persistent data storage. All game state is held in memory and lost when you close the app.

Third-Party Services

The Game does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The Game does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13. There is no user input of personal data at any point.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
