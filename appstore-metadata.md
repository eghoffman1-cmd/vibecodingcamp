## Description (4000 chars max)
Be Anything is a top-down city simulation where YOU decide who you are.

Roll into a living, breathing city and take on one of 8 unique roles — each with its own goals, tools, and story playing out in real time.

**CHOOSE YOUR ROLE**
🚔 Police Officer — Patrol the streets, chase robbers, and keep the city safe
🦹 Robber — Hit banks, jewelry stores, and convenience stores. Stay off the radar
🚒 Firefighter — Race to blazes before the city burns
🚑 Paramedic — Stabilise patients and race them to hospital
🍳 Chef — Keep the diner running through the dinner rush
🚕 Taxi Driver — Ferry passengers across town as fast as you can
🔧 Mechanic — Fix broken-down vehicles around the city
📰 Journalist — Chase breaking news before deadline

**FEATURES**
• Fully simulated city — cops chase robbers, robberies happen in real time, NPCs go about their lives
• Drive any car — jack vehicles or buy from the garage
• Phone system — call a taxi, order food, request backup, fence loot
• Dynamic tasks — earn money and rep completing shift objectives
• Minimap navigation — always know where you are
• 8 playable jobs with unique mechanics
• Endless replayability — reroll your job anytime

No ads. No in-app purchases. Just a city to explore.
